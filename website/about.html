About Cross the Line

Growing up in a small town with big musical ambitions, Cross The Line formed in 2013 at Marine City High School. Cross The Line released their first single Look At Me in August 2013 with producer B.J. Perry of The Product. Immediately following the release, they received a great response from their hometown of Marine City. In October 2013, Look At Me won the Banana 101.5 Flinttown Throwdown battle of the bands, and that was just the beginning of their incredible journey. Throughout a year of high school, Cross The Line recorded and released their EP Revolt August 2014. Their single This Year was showcased on the 89x radio station and is still getting radio time every week, and it is also being featured at the Detroit Red Wings game on November 16th. With numerous shows being scheduled around Detroit (including St. Andrews Hall), new material being written, and a summer tour to prepare for, the next year will be busy for Cross the Line.
